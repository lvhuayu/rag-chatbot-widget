Frequently Asked Questions

What is RAG?
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is an AI technique that combines retrieval of relevant documents with generative models to provide more accurate and grounded responses.
How do I use this chatbot?
Type your question in the chat window. The chatbot will use the content on this page and its backend knowledge to answer.
Can I ingest new content?
Yes, if enabled, you can ingest new content for the chatbot to use in its responses.
